About me

Hello! I am a Korean national living in Manchester. I am originally from Busan, and lived there for 25 years. I decided to move to England and I have been here since March 2019 and l love living in England.
I have experience in teaching and mentoring foreign exchange students whilst studying for my undergraduate degree in Korea. I taught students from Japan, Vietnam, Norway, Malaysia, Sweden and China. I have 10 regular students who continue to progress at the moment. I love seeing this progressing!

About lesson

I want to teach you about the Korean language alongside culture and history etc. I can teach you Korean depend on your interest of Korean food/ K-pop/K-drama/ Korean history ... I will give you material depend on your level of Korean. As I struggled to learn to English I know that learning a new language can be difficult and needs to be Fun! My classes are fun, engaging and tailored to meet your needs.
